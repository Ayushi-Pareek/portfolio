About

I'm a senior product designer based in Bengaluru, with five years working on developer tools: observability platforms, static analysis, and low-code infrastructure. I learn how a product actually works before I touch a screen. That shows up in the work.

I've built design systems from scratch, designed for novel problem spaces like AI agent observability, and shipped with small engineering-led teams where everyone has opinions and speed matters. I move well in that environment.

Outside of design, I make things with paper and cardboard. Notebooks, miniatures, furniture. Turns out building physical systems from scratch and building design systems aren't that different.
