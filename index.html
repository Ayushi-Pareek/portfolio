About

I'm a senior product designer based in Bengaluru. Five years, one focus: developer tools. I've worked across observability, static code analysis, and low-code platforms. I learn how a product actually works before I touch a screen. That shows up in the work.

I've built design systems from scratch, designed for novel problem spaces like AI agent observability, and shipped with small engineering-led teams where everyone has opinions and speed matters. I move well in that environment.
