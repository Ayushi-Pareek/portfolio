Overview

A log analytics platform built for engineers who can't afford guesswork

Parseable is a high-performance log analytics and observability platform. When I joined, the product needed a designer to own it end-to-end, from the core querying experience through to a completely novel problem space: AI agent observability.

I was the sole designer working directly with the engineering team and founders, responsible for everything from information architecture and workflow definition to the final shipped product.
